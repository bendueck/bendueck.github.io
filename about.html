About


This page is supposed to be about me. My name is Ben. I was born in Maryland, USA and briefly lived in Heidelberg, Germany and Alabama, USA. But I grew up in Tsawwassen, British Columbia Canada. I graduated from high school at Richmond Christian School in 2019. Then I studied at Trinity Western University for one year. Then, during the pandemic, I worked as a lifeguard at the City of Delta and camp counsellor at Camp Cascades in Yelm, Washington. I also started producing music using Ableton Live. In 2021, I moved to Edmonton, Alberta and currently am a Business Student at The King's University. I am graduating in May 2024. That's my life!
