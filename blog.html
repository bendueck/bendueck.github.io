Here is an example of one of my hilarious blog posts! In this blog, I will be talking about the best places to eat in Edmonton. Check out the link below to visit these dope places. First up, for breakfast, is OEB. This is a charming brunch spot with two downtown locations (12040 124 St and 10174 100a St NW) as well as a Southside location (5133 Mullen Rd). The second is Burger's Priest. This is a must-try but also a "once-in-a-while" restaurant due to the HUGE burgers, guaranteed to satisfy your fast food craving for a week! They have one downtown location (10148 109 St NW) and one Southside location (2125 98 St NW). Last but not least is MEAT (8216 104 St NW). This place is bussin'! They've got meat. Check it out because it is one of my favourite Edmonton restaurants. Bonus: go with friends and get a platter so you can try multiple things. View the pros and cons of each restaurant below.


Pros Cons
OEB Many locations. Good food. Only open until 3pm.
Burger's Priest Edmonton staple. Fun vibes. Can be greasy.
